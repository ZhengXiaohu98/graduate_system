New Student Tutorial

Change Password:

  • Step 1: Go to Setting
  • Step 2: Click On "Change Password"
  • Step 3: Enter Your Old and New Password
  • Step 4: Click On Button "Update Password"
  • Note: New User Must Change Your Password!!


Add Course:

  • Step 1: Go to Search Course
  • Step 2: Make your selection on Term/Course/Day/Time/Status
  • Step 3: Choose a course from search result
  • Step 4: Add the course to your schedule
  • Note: You can only add courses in Class Registration/Special Registration Period


Drop Course:

  • Step 1: Go to Course Management
  • Step 2: Click on "Drop"
  • Step 3: Confirm your "Drop"
  • Note: You cannot drop course in Grading Period


Review Course:

  • Step 1: Go to Current Information
  • Step 2: Click on "Review"
  • Step 3: Rating the course
  • Step 4: Write your review in description
  • Step 5: Submit you review
  • Note: You cannot drop course in Grading Period


Complain a student/Instructor:

  • Step 1: Go to Complain
  • Step 2: Choose complain type (either Studeng or Instructor)
  • Step 3: Choose Name of Student/Instructor
  • Step 4: Write your complaining message in description
  • Step 5: Submit your complain
  • Note: You can only complain the student or instructor in your current taking course


View Course History:

  • Step 1: Go to Academic Record
  • Step 2: Click On "Course History"


Apply for Graduate:

  • Step 1: Go to Academic Record
  • Step 2: Click on "Apply Graduation"
  • Step 3: Click on button "Apply Graduate" to submit your application
  • Step 4: Confirm your appliation
  • Note: You must complete 8 classes in order to apply graduation
